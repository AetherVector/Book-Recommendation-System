Popularity:

A popularity-based recommendation system is a simple approach where items are recommended based on their overall popularity or popularity within a certain group. The basic idea is to suggest items that are already popular among users or have received a high number of interactions.

Content-Based:

A content-based recommendation system suggests items to users based on the characteristics or features of the items and the preferences of the users. Instead of relying on user-item interactions, it focuses on the attributes of the items and the user's preferences derived from their past interactions

Collabrative filtered

Collaborative filtering is a recommendation system technique that makes automatic predictions (filtering) about the preferences of a user by collecting preferences and information from many users (collaborating). The underlying idea is that users who have agreed in the past tend to agree in the future.
